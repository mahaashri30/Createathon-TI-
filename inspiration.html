How to Steal Your Favorite Celeb’s Style: Get the Look of Zendaya Zendaya has become an undeniable fashion icon, known for her bold, fearless style. Whether on the red carpet or running errands, her looks are always on point. Here’s how you can steal her style: Bold Tailoring: Zendaya loves a well-tailored suit, often pairing them with unexpected elements like crop tops or sneakers. To recreate this look, find a sharply tailored blazer and trousers, and add a fun twist with a graphic tee or casual sneakers. Maxi Dresses with a Twist: From flowing gowns to sleek satin pieces, Zendaya knows how to wear a maxi dress. Look for dresses with unique cuts or vibrant prints, and finish with strappy heels for a show-stopping look. Monochrome Magic: Zendaya often embraces monochrome looks, wearing one color head-to-toe. To replicate this, try an all-black or all-white ensemble and play with textures to add depth to your outfit. Statement Accessories: From oversized hats to dramatic earrings, Zendaya knows how to accessorize. Don’t shy away from bold accessories that make your outfit pop! Zendaya’s fashion is all about confidence, individuality, and fun—so don’t be afraid to experiment and make bold choices in your own wardrobe!

1. Classic Red Carpet Look
Celebrity Inspiration: Zendaya

Outfit: Zendaya is known for her elegant and timeless red carpet outfits. A classic look for Zendaya includes a floor-length gown with intricate details such as sequins, satin, or velvet. Often in bold, solid colors like red or deep jewel tones, her dresses hug the body and feature dramatic necklines, cutouts, or flowy skirts.
Footwear: Stiletto heels or strappy sandals, typically in metallic or nude tones to elongate the legs.
Accessories: Statement jewelry like chandelier earrings, layered necklaces, or a bold cuff bracelet.
Hair: Sleek and polished hair, often styled in glamorous waves, a smooth updo, or a sophisticated ponytail.
Makeup: Bold eye makeup with smoky eyeshadow, long lashes, and a bold lip color like deep red or berry.

2. Casual Street Style Look
Celebrity Inspiration: Kendall Jenner

Outfit: Kendall often showcases an effortless, model-off-duty look. Think high-waisted jeans or leather pants paired with a crop top or oversized T-shirt. Her style often features neutral tones or monochrome looks (like all black or beige).
Footwear: White sneakers, boots, or chunky sandals.
Accessories: Sunglasses, small handbags, and gold or silver jewelry (think layered necklaces and dainty rings).
Hair: Kendall often rocks a sleek, natural style with her hair down or in a ponytail.
Makeup: Natural makeup with a focus on glowing skin, light foundation, and nude lips.

3. Power Suit Look
Celebrity Inspiration: Blake Lively

Outfit: Blake Lively frequently stuns in powerful, feminine suits. A tailored blazer with matching trousers or a pencil skirt. These outfits are often in bold colors like red, deep green, or metallic tones and can feature strong shoulders or peplum waists for extra flair.
Footwear: Pointed-toe heels or pumps.
Accessories: Statement earrings or a bold necklace. Often, she’ll wear a classic watch or carry a structured handbag.
Hair: Blake’s hair is usually styled in soft waves or voluminous curls, adding an element of glamour to her sophisticated look.
Makeup: Bold lips (often in a red or nude shade), defined eyes, and glowing skin.

4. Traditional Bollywood Glam
Celebrity Inspiration: Deepika Padukone

Outfit: Deepika often stuns in traditional sarees, lehengas, or anarkalis for Bollywood events. She is known for wearing grand sarees in luxurious fabrics like silk, chiffon, or georgette with intricate embroidery or embellishments. A bold color palette such as red, gold, and royal blue is often seen in her outfits.
Footwear: Heeled sandals or embellished flats to complement the saree or lehenga.
Accessories: Heavy jewelry like statement earrings, maang tikka, and chunky bangles. Deepika often chooses gold jewelry to complement her ethnic looks.
Hair: Elegant updos or long waves with side-parted hairstyles.
Makeup: Bold makeup with a well-defined face—smoky eyes, red lips, and dewy skin.

5. Contemporary Indo-Western Look
Celebrity Inspiration: Priyanka Chopra Jonas

Outfit: Priyanka is often seen blending Indian and Western styles, especially in outfits like lehenga skirts with cropped tops, saree-gowns, or contemporary sarees. For example, she’s worn sarees with modern blouses or paired lehenga skirts with blazers.
Footwear: High heels or strappy sandals.
Accessories: Statement earrings, rings, or a simple clutch bag.
Hair: Sleek straight hair or soft waves. Priyanka often opts for chic, modern hairstyles that complement her western outfits.
Makeup: Glowing skin with defined brows, neutral eyeshadow, and nude lips. Her makeup often strikes a balance between bold and natural.
