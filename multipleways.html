MULTIPLE WAYS OF STYLING ! - WOMEN AND MEN


STYLE FOR WOMEN




1. Casual Look

  • Bottoms: Pair with jeans, shorts, or leggings.
  • Footwear: Sneakers, sandals, or casual flats.
  • Accessories: Crossbody bag, simple jewelry (like studs or a watch).
  • Layering: Tuck the shirt in or leave it untucked, or tie a knot at the waist for a relaxed vibe.
  • Hair & Makeup: Messy bun or ponytail, with minimal makeup for a natural look.






    • 2. Tucked-In for a Polished Look
  • Bottoms: High-waisted trousers, skirts, or tailored pants.
  • Footwear: Loafers, ballet flats, or block heels
  • Accessories: A belt to accentuate the waist, a structured handbag, and simple jewelry.
  • Layering: Tuck the shirt in or leave it untucked, or tie a knot at the waist for a relaxed vibe.
  • Hair & Makeup: Sleek ponytail or straight hair, with light makeup (neutral eyes and a soft lip).






    • 3. Layered with a Cardigan or Blazer
  • Bottoms: Skinny jeans, leggings, or a pencil skirt.
  • Footwear: Boots, loafers, or heels.
  • Accessories: Layer with a cardigan, blazer, or denim jacket. Add a scarf and structured bag.
  • Hair & Makeup: Loose waves or a neat updo with bold lipstick.
  • Bonus Tip: Roll up the sleeves of your shirt for a more relaxed, stylish touch.
    •





    4. Over a Dress
  • Bottoms: Pair with a midi or maxi dress for a layered effect.
  • Footwear: Heels, wedges, or ankle boots.
  • Accessories: A belt to define your waist, a chic handbag.
  • Layering: Wear the shirt open like a lightweight jacket over a dress.
  • Hair & Makeup: Soft curls or a sleek ponytail, and subtle makeup.
    •





    5. Oversized Look
  • Bottoms: Leggings, skinny jeans, or bike shorts
  • Footwear: Chunky sneakers or slides.
  • Accessories: Bold sunglasses and a small crossbody bag.
  • Layering: Wear the shirt oversized, either tucked in slightly in the front or left loose
  • Hair & Makeup: Messy bun, ponytail, or sleek low bun, with a bold lip or graphic eyeliner for a trendy touch. .
    •





    STYLE FOR MEN




    1. Casual Day Look

  • Bottoms: Pair with jeans, chinos, or shorts.
  • Footwear: Sneakers, loafers, or casual slip-ons.
  • Accessories: A simple watch or bracelet, and a casual backpack or messenger bag.
  • Layering: Keep the shirt untucked for a relaxed, effortless vibe.
  • Hair & Makeup: Natural hairstyle, or a messy, tousled look. Minimal or no makeup for a fresh and natural appearance.






    • 2. Smart-Casual Look
  • Bottoms: Slim-fit chinos or dark jeans.
  • Footwear: Desert boots, loafers, or brogues.
  • Accessories: A leather belt, a watch, and a slim briefcase or messenger bag.
  • Layering: Tuck the shirt into your chinos or jeans for a neat, polished appearance. Optionally, throw on a cardigan or a blazer for added style.
  • Hair & Makeup: Neat, combed hair or a side part.Fresh face with minimal grooming products.
    •





    3. Weekend Chill Look
  • Bottoms: Distressed jeans, shorts, or joggers.
  • Footwear: Sneakers or slip-on shoes.
  • Accessories: A beanie or snapback, and a crossbody bag.
  • Layering: Leave the shirt untucked or tie it at the waist for a more relaxed look. You can also throw on a denim or bomber jacket.
  • Hair & Makeup: Messy or styled in a casual, laid-back way.None or a light touch of grooming products for a natural look.
    •





    4. Professional Look
  • Bottoms: Well-fitted chinos or dress pants.
  • Footwear: Leather loafers, derby shoes, or clean dress shoes.
  • Accessories: A leather belt, sleek watch, and briefcase or work bag.
  • Layering: Tuck the shirt in, and add a cardigan, blazer, or sport coat for a more professional look.
  • Hair & Makeup: Neatly styled with a side part or a clean comb-over.
    •





    5. Summer Casual Look
  • Bottoms: Lightweight chinos, shorts, or linen pants.
  • Footwear: Sandals, espadrilles, or slip-on shoes.
  • Accessories: Sunglasses, a cap, and a beach bag.
  • Layering: Wear the shirt untucked and unbuttoned for a laid-back vibe. You could also roll up the sleeves to add some extra flair.
  • Hair & Makeup: Casual, beachy style or short and neat.Light sunscreen and grooming for a fresh summer look.
